Have you ever been in trouble in the middle of a battle?

If you answered yes, then I have some great news for you. I have 4 things that will help you fix your troubles

1. Retreat

The easiest way to get out of trouble is to retreat. This allows you to move backwards to a place you might be able to defend better

2. Kamikaze

If retreating does not work and you have nowhere else to go, you should let your soldiers begin to kamikaze to sacrifice themselves so you can try to win the battle.

3. Nuclear Weapons

If your soldiers do not want to kamikaze or you are down to your last few remaining soldiers, then you should resort to nuclear options.

4. Surrender

If somehow did not win the battle after using nuclear weapons, you should probably surrender, but remeber you just violated many international laws by using nuclear weapons, so run and hide and do not let them catch you
